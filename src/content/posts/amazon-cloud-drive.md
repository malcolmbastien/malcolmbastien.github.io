[onethingwell](http://onethingwell.org/post/4183562770/amazon-cloud-drive):

5 GB of free storage. 20 GB for $20 per annum, 50 GB for $50, and so on, all the way up to a 1000 GB for $1000.

The real story, though, is the fact that MP3s purchased from Amazon are stored for free, and can be streamed via the [Amazon Cloud Player](http://www.amazon.com/b/?ie=UTF8&node=2658409011).

In other words, Amazon have beaten Apple to the punch in offering music in the cloud.
